About
AAD™ (Across All Domains Test) is an open benchmark designed to evaluate frontier artificial intelligence systems across a wide range of intellectual capabilities.
Unlike benchmarks focused on a single discipline, AAD evaluates models across numerous domains while emphasizing reasoning, knowledge application, reliability, problem solving, and instruction following.
